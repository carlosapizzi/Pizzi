How I got in love with cooking

Since I was a little kid I went every summer to Orlando to my Grandma's house.

My Grandma used to pick me up every day and sat me on the kitchen table and would try to teach me a new recipe.

Every day I would try to cook with her and that's when I started loving the kitchen.

She surprised me with different types of dishes; lunch, dessert, supper. This made me really exited.

The last time I went to her house I made her some of her rice and beans and she was very suprised on how close they tasted to hers.

Fun fact... Thanks to her I only know how to cook for more than 4 people I can't make a dish for one pearson.

So now you know a little about my story.
